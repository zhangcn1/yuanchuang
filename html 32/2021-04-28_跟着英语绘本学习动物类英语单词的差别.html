跟着英语绘本学习动物类英语单词的差别

原创J家姐妹花J家姐妹花2021-04-28 16:37:00

一年前，我写过还记得A puma at large吗？（文末有彩蛋）中，分析了Cheetah，Jaguar，Leopard，Panther，Puma都作为豹子，有什么区别。


随着孩子的成长，英语词汇不断积累，不免要在阅读时来询问我：“妈妈，这个单词和之前学过的blabla有什么不同呀？”，我本来一直以为自己的英语还不错，没想到一次次被问倒。


今天想汇总起来，做一个动物类近义词的趣味学习分享。当然，我还是要强调，我们必须多使用原版的英语材料来进行词汇的辨析，因为翻译成中文真的体会不出其中的差别。还有，没有必要一口吃成个大胖子，不是生物专业的我用中文也解释不出这么多区别呀，一下子记不住没有关系，学习外语，就是要细水长流，日积月累。


1、作为鳄鱼的crocodile和alligator有什么区别？



教材中提到，crocodile的口鼻处（snout）和牙齿都要长于alligator。Alligator颜色深，crocodile颜色浅。


（颜色对比）


我认为，最明显的、最容易记住的就是形状区分这一点，crocodile是V型snout，而alligator是U型snout。


（图片摘自wikihow）


给大家做一个测试，在《May I please have a cookie》中，主人公Alfie是一只crocodile还是一只alligator呢？

根据snout的形状来推测，我觉得Alfie应该是一只crocodile吧：）


再多查阅一些科普知识（也许很难记住），如何区分crocodile和alligator，一是看上下颚闭合时候，下颚的牙齿是否明显暴露在外面且盖在上颚上。暴露的话就是crocodile，不暴露的就是alligator；二是看水域，只要生活在咸水里的肯定是crocodile，淡水里即有可能是alligator也有可能是crocodile；三是看地理分布，alligator只分布在美洲和中国；crocodile分布广泛。


2、“章鱼、鱿鱼、八爪鱼、乌贼”这种我连中文都傻傻分不清楚，squid，octopus，calamari，cuttlefish之间的差别是什么？


The octopus

The main distinction octopus has from squid and cuttlefish is that, as their name implies, they have eight tentacles, whereas cuttlefish and squid have ten. Also, octopuses are a lot bigger than squid and cuttlefish, and they have longer and larger tentacles.


The second difference among octopus, squid and cuttlefish is that the octopus is the only one who doesn’t have a shell. Squid and cuttlefish have an internal “structure” to protect them. This makes the octopus more flexible.


Another big difference between those three is that octopus is able to change its skin color and use it as a camouflage.


Octopus，从其词根octo→八，pus→足，连在一起翻译成中文就是八爪鱼。八爪鱼最为柔软，因为相较于squid和cuttlefish，是唯一没有“壳”的。八爪鱼还会变色来保护自己。


（octopus）

推荐一本名为《An octopus is amazing》的绘本，里面提及不少octopus的特点，趣味容易记。



The squid

Compared to octopus, squid have eight arms and two tentacles that they use to catch their prey. Squid often live in pairs while octopus mostly live alone.

Squid is smaller and usually tougher than octopus or cuttlefish due to its internal shell and the way it eats, using only two tentacles.

It is squid that is used to make the famous “calamari rings.” In fact, calamari is “squid” in Italian.


Squid做成菜以后，变成了鱿鱼圈（calamari ring）。


（squid & calamari ring）


The cuttlefish

The main difference between cuttlefish and the two others is their shape and looks. The fact that cuttlefish have an internal shell called “cuttlebone” make them the slowest and least flexible of the three.
If you look at them, octopus and squid are fast predators, whereas cuttlefish are slower. They move using wings on the sides of their bodies.


Another way to tell the difference is by looking at their eyes. Octopus and squid have rounded eyes, but cuttlefish have eyes in the shape of a W.


Finally, looking at the cuttlefish body, they are fatter and rounder whereas squid has a more elongated body also called a “tube.” Cuttlefish also is more meaty with a thicker body.


Cuttlefish的眼睛呈W形，身体呈筒形，更圆润一些。


（cuttlefish）


根据英语科普释义，我找到一张cuttlefish，squid，octopus并列在一起的比较图，再加上科普知识的查阅如下：



乌贼/墨鱼 (Cuttlefish) 其实不是鱼，是一种软体动物，不过壳退化变成了内骨骼。身体是长筒形的，十爪。墨鱼是乌贼的俗称，也叫墨斗鱼，身上有不均的黑斑。


鱿鱼/枪乌贼 (Squid) 跟乌贼是同一目，十爪，同样的长筒形，身体更狭长些，有点儿标枪（ bullet-shaped head）的样子，所以也叫枪乌贼。内骨骼很薄。


章鱼/八爪鱼 (Octopus) 完全是软体的，身体是圆形（bag-like head），八爪。


Calamari是Squid作为食材的名字，类似pork，beef这种，因此我们平时爱吃的Calamari Ring，就叫鱿鱼圈。


3、灵长类动物gorilla，chimpanzee，ape，orangutan的区别呢？


Chimpanzees are more intelligent than gorillas and orangutans. They have large ears and dark hair. They live in groups, and can walk with two feet for a short time.

Orangutans are smaller than gorillas, but larger than chimpanzees. They are reddish to dark brown in colour. The males have large cheek pads. Unlike gorillas and chimpanzees, they do not live in social groups.

Gorillas are very strong, and larger than twice the size of a chimpanzee. Their bodies are covered with long dark hair. They live in groups.


有学姐推荐我，关于猩猩们的比较，具体可以去上海自然博物馆“人和人的近亲”处去观察对比。



Ape指类人猿，是个总称，可以指这三种大猿（除人以外）。


Gorilla是大猩猩, 现存东部大猩猩和西部大猩猩，自然博物馆中的是西部大猩猩。体型巨大，是黑猩猩的两倍。



在Anthony Browne的绘本《Gorilla》中，大猩猩身材巨大，在安娜的梦中陪伴安娜完成想和爸爸一起度过的快乐的一天。


（看到大猩猩的身材魁梧庞大了吧）


Orangutan是红毛猩猩，现存只在东南亚，orangu是马来语，指“树林中的人”。


黑猩猩就是Chimpanzee，是人类最近的近亲，所以也最聪明，大概相当于人7-10岁的智力。



4、同属于兔子的Rabbit, Bunny，Hare有什么区别？


Hares look like rabbits, but they are larger and run faster than rabbits.
Hares live in open fields, and their babies are born with fur and open eyes.

Rabbits live in trees, and they like to dig holes. They are born with no fur and closed eyes.

Bunny is the nickname of a rabbit or hare.

Rabbit泛指兔子全体，尤其是普通的家兔。

比如说大家都很喜欢的英国经典故事书彼得兔就是Peter Rabbit。


Hare是野兔，体型较大。



《Guess how much I love you》里的nutbrown hare就是野兔。



Bunny是“小兔子”的意思。绘本《I am a bunny》和 《Pat the bunny》里的小兔子就是bunny。Bunny和rabbit是同一种动物。
