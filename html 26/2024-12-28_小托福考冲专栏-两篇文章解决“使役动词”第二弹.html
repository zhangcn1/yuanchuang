上次分享了let，make，have的用法（回看上期）

本篇来一起看看get，leave，keep吧～

老习惯，先看题目～选自《全真模拟题》Test 5, Test 8

get 和leave 用法的重合度非常高，

大家关注点放在get和leave 动词本身的含义上

get 为普通的“使...变得”

leave 含有“留有”的意思

get

  • get sb./sth. to do  让某人/某物做...

e.g. He tried his best to get him to understand.他用尽努力使他明白

  • get sb. /sth. done   让某人/某事被...

e.g.  He'll never get all this work finished.这么多的工作他怎么也干不完。

  • get sb./sth. adj. 让某物/某人变得...

e.g. She soon got herself ready for the meeting.她很快使自己做好了开会的准备。

邀请你来试一试





He got his sister ________ him with his homework. (help)

He couldn't get the computer ____________. (work)

Yoyo tried to get the papers ____________. (correct) 

We promised to get our homework _________. (do)

Don't get your dress ___________. (dirt)





答案：to help; to work; corrected; done; dirty

leave  

看看是不是在搭配上与get很相似

leave sb./sth. to do 留某人做某事 

leave sb./sth. + adj.  把某物以某种状态留在那儿 

举例

leave the door closed

leave the door open

leave sb./sth. doing 让某人/某物一直做某事

邀请你来试一试





Don't leave your homework _____________. (finish) 

Please don't leave me _____________. 请不要让我独自一人。 

My mother leaves me _____________ the homework. (do) 





答案：unfinished；alone；to do

再来看看下面两题





My mother gets me ___________ the homework all through the day. (do)

 My mother leaves the washing machine _____________ all day. (work)





感到熟悉吗？有梦回上一篇吗？

当出现表示一段时间的状语，例如all day， the whole afternoon等，强调的是使某人一直做某事，此时用doing

get sb. doing 使某人一直做

leave sb. doing 留有某人一直做

最后一个，来到keep啦

keep的特色性也很强，因为本身表示“保持，持续”，则非谓语搭配用doing

  • keep sb. /sth. + adj. 使某人/某物保持...的状态

  • keep sb/ sth. doing 让某人/某物一直做某事


邀请你来试一试





Please keep the door_________. (open)/ _________ (close).

We should do regular exercise to keep _______(us) __________ (health)

My mother keeps me _________ the homework all day long. (do)





答案：open，closed；ourselves，healthy；doing

画画格子，大家有没有发现规律，除了前两行，是需要重点区分

“v. sb. do ” or “v. sb. to do”？

其他大部分是相似的
